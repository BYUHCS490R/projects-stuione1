1. Title, Topic, and Why I Picked It

Title: Pacific Island Food & Culture

Topic: My website will be about foods from the Pacific Islands and how they connect to culture and family.

I chose this topic because I grew up around this stuff and it’s something I actually know about. Food is a big part of my family life, so this is something I feel good building a project on.

2. What Will Be On Each Page

Home Page:

  • Short intro about Pacific Island culture
  • Why food matters
  • Photos and links to other pages

Page 1: History & Culture

  • Some background on Pacific Islands
  • How food is used in traditions
  • Food in family gatherings

Page 2: Popular Dishes (Table Page)

  • Different well-known island dishes
  • Pictures
  • A table comparing dishes

Page 3: Ingredients & Sustainability

  • Main ingredients like taro, coconut, fish, breadfruit
  • Traditional cooking/farming ideas
  • How food connects to the land

Page 4: Gallery & Video

  • Photo gallery
  • One YouTube video about island food
  • Short write-up about the video

Page 5: Recipe/Feedback Form

  • Form people can fill out
  • 4+ fields (name, email, island, favorite dish, message)
  • A confirmation page after they submit

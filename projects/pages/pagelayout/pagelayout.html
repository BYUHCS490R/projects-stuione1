Surfing in Hawaii is one of my favorite things to do. The waves change every day, so there is always something new to learn. Paddling out early helps me clear my head and start the day right.

My favorite place to surf is Kahana Bay. When it’s breaking, the waves run long and work perfect for my longboard. It’s even better because my wife surfs too, and it’s something we can enjoy together.

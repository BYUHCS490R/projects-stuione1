Explore the Islands of the Pacific

This site is a simple guide to some of my favorite islands in the Pacific. It is not a full travel site, but more like a personal project that shares places I think are beautiful and worth learning about.

Hawaiʻi

Hawaiʻi is known for surfing, warm beaches, and a mix of modern city life and island traditions. It is one of the easiest Pacific islands to visit and a popular first stop.

Fiji

Fiji has clear water, coral reefs, and small islands that feel calm and quiet. It is a good place for snorkeling and slower, relaxed trips.

Tonga

Tonga is called the Friendly Islands and is known for strong culture, family, and connections to the ocean. It is less busy than many tourist spots.
